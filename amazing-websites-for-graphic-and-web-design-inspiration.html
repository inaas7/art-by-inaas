Amazing Websites for Graphic and Web Design Inspiration

September 5, 2020 â”‚ By : Inaas Asad

If you're having a designer's block, you should definitely read this post.

I find inspiration in myself.

Just kidding. I'm definitely not like that. I find inspiration everywhere ranging from random websites to fascinating people. I want to share some of the coolest websites that I use to find more specifically, graphic and web design inspiration.

1. Designspiration

Designspiration works like Pinterest, but its content is focused on design. It has endless ideas on various different topics including colors, compositions and themes. It's pretty easy to get lost for hours while surfing through this website. Whether you're looking for ideas for your next website or you simply want to create a poster to advertise your school club, this website would help you out.


2. CodePen

CodePen is a huge community of 1.8 million+ front-end designers and developers. It's a platform where people share ideas, build stunning websites and find inspiration. This has to be one of my favorite websites. When I first discovered it, I cannot put into words how much ideas were poured into my brain after just one visit. You can find astounding work ranging from simple patterns to entrancing animations.


3. A List Apart

This website has incredible mix of articles for web layouts and typography, graphic design, interface design, user experience design, illustration, photography, and artwork. It gives both creative and technical approaches to creating great designs.


4. abdz

Abdz keeps you updated with unique articles on the current design trends. What's great about it is that it posts articles on diverse topics. You'll find articles on new classes graphic designers have released, interesting tools to use, effects to add into your work, and tonnes more.


5. Dribbble

As their website advertises, "Dribbble is the leading destination to find & showcase creative work and home to the world's best design professionals." The website is overflowing with talent and showcases exceptional work in various areas such as illustration, animation, typography and web design. It even hosts interactive design workshops and has a blog filled with amazing tutorials and interviews with design industry leaders. It's a fascinating website that no one should miss.


6. Canva

Canva offers thousands of templates of various types that are free to download and use. It even lets you customize templates, and you don't even need any graphic design experience to be able to do so. You can browse designs in countless categories including websites, posters, Instagram posts, infographics, logos and dozens more.


7. UpLabs

UpLabs is a platform where you can explore high-quality design resources in web design, icons, illustrations, themes and more. The designs are extremely unique and eye-catching. A lot of them are even free to download and use in your own projects. It's a captivating website.


8. Freebiesbugs

As the name suggests, this website boasts absolutely free designs for creative people. The templates, fonts, illustrations, icons, etc. are all extraordinary and unique. The best part about it is that alongside getting inspiration, you get to use the designs for yourself too.
