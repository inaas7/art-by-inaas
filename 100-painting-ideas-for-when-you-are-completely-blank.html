100 Painting Ideas for when You are Completely Blank

June 4, 2020 â”‚ By : Inaas Asad

Can't think of a single idea to convert into a painting? Fear not, for you've come to the right place.

We've all been there. You're sitting in front of your workplace with your paints and brushes scattered and with your eyeballs glued to your empty canvas. You're listening to some amazing music through your brand new headphones, except your brain is a blank canvas itself. It's pretty frustrating when not a single idea seems to be making its way to your head. If that's the case, it's your lucky day. You just discovered a diverse list of 100 ideas that you can feed into that brain of yours.

  1. 1) A scene from your favorite movie (e.g. "Alice in Wonderland" or "Titanic")
  2. 2) A sunrise
  3. 3) A portrait of your favorite celebrity
  4. 4) Your favorite animal
  5. 5) The galaxy (Try out fluid art to paint a galaxy. It's probably the easiest way to do it.)
  6. 6) A landmark from your favorite country (perhaps the Eiffel tower or the Colosseum)
  7. 7) A sunset
  8. 8) A rain scene
  9. 9) A fruit bowl (Try arranging real fruits on a table and transferring what you see onto the canvas.)
  10. 10) Fluffy clouds (Step up your game and add a person carrying out an activity in the clouds.)
  11. 11) What freedom looks like (Use symbolism.)
  12. 12) A forest scene
  13. 13) Something that is typical of your country
  14. 14) Time (You can paint a fancy clock slowly being shattered to pieces. Be creative.)
  15. 15) Roses over a fence
